What we cover

Eight situations people deal with constantly — and rarely fight.

Security deposit

Landlord kept it, went silent, or missed the legal deadline to return it.

Refund owed

A business took payment and never delivered, or won't honor its own return policy.

Unpaid final wages

Last paycheck is late or short, past your state's required payout window.

Property damage

Someone damaged your property and won't cover the cost to repair it.

Breach of contract

A signed agreement was broken, and you need it documented before pursuing further.

Unpaid invoice

You did the work or delivered the goods. Payment never came.

Debt collector overreach

Harassment, wrong amounts, or contact after you've asked them to stop.

General demand

Doesn't fit a category above, but you need it in writing and on the record regardless.

Every letter includes citations to your state's relevant statute where one applies — so the recipient knows you're not guessing.
